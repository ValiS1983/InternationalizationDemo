The Perfect Tuna Melt

  • 3 (6-ounce) cans solid, water-packed tuna, drained
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cornichons or small kosher dill pickles
  • 3 tablespoons minced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 packed tablespoon minced fresh dill (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons whole-grain mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 slices rye or sourdough bread, cut 1/2-inch thick
  • 8 sandwich slices extra-sharp Cheddar (or 6 ounces shredded)
  • 4 tablespoons softened unsalted butter, plus more as needed

Mix everything (except the cheese and butter) in a medium sized bowl with the drained tuna, mixing with a fork to break up the flakes.

Lay out the bread slices. Divide the tuna mixture onto the slices. Top each bread slice with the cheddar cheese.

Heat a large pan, on medium heat. Place the sandwiches, buttered side down, onto the pan. Gently spread butter on top of the bread slices. Cook for about 3 minutes.

Use a spoon to lift up the sandwiches and carefully turn them to the other side. Cook for another 3 minutes or so, until the second side is browned.

Easy Harissa Paste

  • 1/2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp caraway seeds
  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, coarsely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
  • 3 hot red chiles, seeded and coarsely chopped
  • 1 1/2 tsp tomato paste
